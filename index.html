About Us

Shaping Young Minds for a Brighter Future

BrainSpark Abacus Academy is dedicated to nurturing young minds through the ancient art of abacus learning, combined with modern teaching methodologies.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, BrainSpark Abacus Academy began with a simple vision: to make mental mathematics fun, engaging, and accessible to every child. What started as a single classroom has now grown into a network of centers across the city, transforming the lives of thousands of young learners.

Our unique approach combines traditional abacus techniques with gamified learning, ensuring that every child not only masters mental math but also develops critical thinking, concentration, and confidence.
